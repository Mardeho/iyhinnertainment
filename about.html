About

IYH Innertainment is a media-driven event house founded by Jori O’Neale. We blend storytelling, music, and culture to create experiences that move people — in the room and on the record.

We produce podcasts, host live events, and deliver DJ services for organizations, churches, and community spaces who care about atmosphere and message.
